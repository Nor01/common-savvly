BENEFITS & FEATURES

  • You decide when you can get access to your Savvly funds by selecting your ideal payout date age.
  • No minimum investments: Invest $10 or $1m or more.
  • Flexible funding: choose monthly installments or one-time deposits whichever best fits your situation. You can draw on your existing retirement accounts (401k, IRA, etc.).
  • Powered by the sharing economy: yielding unprecedented returns by combining asset sharing with the concept of longevity risk.
